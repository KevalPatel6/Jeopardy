Jeopardy!

Instructions

  • After clicking, you'll be greeted by the Jeopardy! game board, complete with categories and clues.
  • Choose a category that piques your interest and click on it to reveal a question.
  • Each category has questions of varying difficulty levels, the higher the value, the more challenging the question. Select a question to view the clue.
  • After answering, reveal the correct answer and select if your were correct or incorrect. Your score will increase with a correct answer and decrease with an incorrect answer.
  • Enjoy the game!
